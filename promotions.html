Frequently Asked Questions

To claim a promotion, simply click on the "Claim Offer" or similar button on the promotion card. You may need to be logged in to your account. Some promotions are applied automatically, while others may require a promo code or opt-in.

A wagering requirement is the number of times you need to bet your bonus amount before you can withdraw any winnings. For example, if you receive a $50 bonus with a 10x wagering requirement, you'll need to place bets totaling $500 before you can withdraw any winnings from the bonus.

Generally, you can only have one active bonus at a time. You'll need to complete the wagering requirements of your current bonus before claiming another one. Some ongoing promotions like the Free Bet Club can run alongside other bonuses.

If you don't meet the wagering requirements within the specified timeframe, the bonus and any winnings derived from it will be forfeited. Always check the time limit for completing wagering requirements in the specific promotion terms.
